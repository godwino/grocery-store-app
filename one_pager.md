# Grocery Store Inventory Optimizer

**Forecast demand, prevent stock-outs, and reduce waste.**

## What it does
- Forecasts demand from your sales history
- Calculates safety stock and reorder points
- Recommends optimal order quantities (EOQ)
- Flags items at risk of stock-out
- Generates reorder lists for purchasing

## How it works
1) Upload inventory (stock, lead time, unit cost)
2) Upload sales history (optional but best)
3) App calculates demand, reorder points, EOQ
4) Get a clear action list + export

## Business impact
- Fewer stock-outs and lost sales
- Less over-buying and waste
- Faster, consistent purchasing decisions
- Better cash flow control

## Who it’s for
Small to mid-size grocery shops and multi-store operators.
